As football team sponsors to Southampton F.C., Virgin Media created a competition which would allow young people to walk on the pitch during half-time of their last game before Christmas. On behalf of RAPP, I was asked to compose an image which would sell this competition to Virgin Media's Twitter following. The final image needed to display an element of Christmas, being on the pitch and say what the competition was.
